Current Toys:

  • Counting By click numbers to see counting-by that number

  • Polygons click numbers to see polygons with that number of sides (note, this toy requires SVG support in the browser)

  • Word List SK wordlist with click-to-say (computer voice currently)
  • Click Fast Click on the spoken words
  • Sub Words Type in word-fragments to see words that contain that word-fragment (note: will load slowly due to the large word-list)
  • Inner Words Type a word to see the words inside it (note: will load slowly due to the large word-list)
